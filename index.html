About Me

My journey into programming began in 2016 with hobby projects and tutorials on Unity. By late 2019, I started taking programming more seriously and enrolled in a Udemy bootcamp to learn the basics. This experience ignited my passion for development.

To further my skills, I joined IronHack Lisbon, where I honed my expertise in the Agile methodology and mastered working with Databases and Front and Back end technologies.

I have now been a developer for a few years and have gained more knowledge and passion for creating outstanding user experiences.
